About Us

Welcome to Sierra, the pulsating heart of BMU's event culture! Since its inception, Sierra has stood at the forefront of orchestrating major events, weaving a tapestry of grandeur that captivates the entire student community. Our diverse range of activities, from internationally acclaimed events to fostering a culture of diplomacy, makes Sierra a dynamic hub for students seeking both intellectual stimulation and creative expression.

Sierra is not just a club; it's a vibrant community that thrives on innovation, collaboration, and the shared pursuit of excellence. Join us on this exhilarating journey where every event is not just an occasion but a celebration of collective brilliance!

Goals & Mission

Cultivate Diplomacy Culture

Sierra aims to promote a culture of diplomacy within BMU. Through our Sierra Diplomacy Forum, we aspire to engage like-minded members in Model United Nations, Youth Parliaments, Mock Debates, and various training workshops, fostering critical thinking and global awareness.

Ignite Entrepreneurial Spirit

Sierra is not just an event organizer but a catalyst for entrepreneurship. Our goal is to inspire and support students in their extracurricular pursuits through initiatives like Money Muffin, empowering them to organize their own fundraisers and realize their dreams.

Elevate Academic and Gaming Excellence

Committed to excellence, Sierra strives to elevate both academic and gaming prowess. We engage in diverse activities, including e-sports, creating a dynamic space for students to explore their interests beyond the traditional academic realm.

EVENTS 

1

TEDx - Today and Tomorrow

TEDx, a dynamic social gathering event, brings live TEDx performances to our community, driven by impactful talks and compelling themes. Each TEDx event is purpose-driven, aiming to inspire and motivate the audience through concise yet powerful speeches. Conducted twice at the university, TEDx has successfully sparked inspiration and initiated a transformative shift in attitudes and perspectives.
2

Money Muffin

Money Muffin is Sierra's crowdfunding initiative, empowering students in need of financial support by organizing food fests. Through this event, students create fundraising opportunities, having successfully hosted this initiative thrice at the university. Money Muffin aims to ignite hope and dreams by raising funds collectively.
3

SIERRA Diplomacy Forum (SDF)

SDF, an integral part of SIERRA BMU, champions the promotion of diplomatic culture through parliamentary debates, MUNs, and mock debates. It serves as a platform to nurture young diplomats, equipping them with problem-solving skills to address real-world issues.
4

SIERRA MUN

A dynamic United Nations simulation cultivating future diplomats. Students engage as delegates and speakers, representing countries to address global issues. This immersive experience hones leadership, public speaking, and problem-solving skills, fostering confidence in diplomatic negotiations.

5

Sierra 1UP

Club Sierra's gaming arm, Sierra 1UP, hosts monthly tournaments for our gaming community. Recently, a LAN event featured Call of Duty Mobile (CODM) and a party game. Open to teams of 5, this hybrid experience, accommodating day scholars, was free of registration fees. Stay tuned for more gaming excitement, including titles like Clash of Royals!
